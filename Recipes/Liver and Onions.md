## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 pounds (ca. 907 g) sliced beef liver
- [ ] 1 ½ buttermilk or cups milk, or as needed
- [ ] ¼ cup butter, divided
- [ ] 2 large Vidalia onions, sliced into rings
- [ ] 2 cups all-purpose flour, or as needed
- [ ] salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Gently rinse liver slices under cold water and pat dry with paper towels. Place in a shallow dish; pour in enough milk to cover. Let stand while preparing onions, or set aside for longer if you have time. Soaking will remove any bitterness.
2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large frying-pan over medium heat. Add onion rings; cook and stir in butter until tender and soft, about 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer onions into a bowl and set the frying-pan aside.
3. Place flour on a plate; season with salt and pepper. Drain milk from liver and coat liver slices in flour mixture.
4. Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter in the same frying-pan; increase heat to medium-high and fry coated liver slices until lightly browned, about 3 to 4 minutes on each side. Return onions to the frying-pan and reduce heat to medium; continue to cook until onions are heated through and liver is slightly pink inside, or longer to suit your taste.
