Having a few homemade and ready-to-use seasoning blends in your pantry is a must! They come in handy for a quick mix and can add a lot of flavour to your dish. You might be wondering why make these seasoning blends like fajita seasoning, ranch seasoning, Italian herb seasoning etc., at home, rather than buy it at the store?

Well, homemade seasoning blends will any day be better than a store-bought one, and there’s nothing like it. Try these seasoning blend recipes at home and you can thank me later! Here are some of my all-time musts, which I have in my pantry.

### Italian Herb Seasoning

Add flavour to your dishes with Italian herb seasoning to use in your pasta sauce, marinades, or roasted veggies!

![[italian-herb.webp]]

**Ingredients:**

- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- 2–3 teaspoons red chilli flakes
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 tablespoons dried basil
- 1 tablespoon dried thyme
- 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
- 2 tablespoons dried marjoram or oregano (Or dried sage, if you do have either)

**To make the seasoning mix:**

Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool, dry place, in an airtight container.

### Fajita Seasoning

A homemade blend to spice up any meat.

**Ingredients:**

- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon cumin powder
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon fine sugar
- 2 teaspoons chilli powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika powder

**To make the seasoning mix:**

Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool, dry place, in an airtight container. Use 1 tablespoon (or more depending on your taste) of homemade fajita seasoning to season 1 pound of meat like chicken, beef, shrimp, or pork.

### Ranch Seasoning

Use this homemade seasoning in dips and dressings.
![[ranch-seasoning.webp]]

**Ingredients:**

- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper powder
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 2 teaspoons dried dill
- ½ teaspoon sea salt

**To make the seasoning mix:**

Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool, dry place, in an airtight container. To make a ranch dressing, combine – Use 1 tablespoons or more of homemade ranch seasoning with 1/2 cup buttermilk, 1/2 cup mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon lemon juice.

Cajun Seasoning

Your go-to-seasoning to flavor meats, seafood, soups, stews and more.

**Ingredients:**

- 1 tablespoon paprika powder (Or smoked paprika powder)
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper powder
- 2 teaspoons black pepper powder
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- 2 teaspoons salt

**To make the seasoning mix:**

Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool, dry place, in an airtight container.

### Taco Seasoning

The next time you make tacos, use this homemade taco seasoning. It is so much better than store bought taco seasoning mix!

![[taco-seasoning-1.webp]]

**Ingredients:**

- 2 teaspoons chilli powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
- ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder

**To make the seasoning mix:**

Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool dry place, in an airtight container. Use this seasoning not just on tacos but also on soups, salads, veggies and more.

### Pizza Seasoning

Perfect spice and herb mix to make the tastiest pizza.

**Ingredients:**

- ¼ cup dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 2 tablespoons dried basil
- 1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon thyme
- 1 tablespoon fennel
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

**To make the seasoning mix:** Into a mixing bowl, add in all the ingredients and give it a mix. Then, is ready to use. Store in a cool, dry place, in an airtight container.

### [[Notes]]

- **How do I store these homemade seasoning blends for a longer shelf-life?** You will need to store these homemade seasoning blends in an airtight container and store it in a cool and dry place.
- **How long do these homemade seasoning blends last?** You can keep these homemade seasoning blends in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months (and they will last this long only if stored in an airtight container).
- **Tip:** If you have made a large batch, you can store it in an airtight container and only keep just a little in a smaller container for frequent use.
