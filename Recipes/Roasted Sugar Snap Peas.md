## Ingredients

- [ ] ½ lb sugar snap peas strings and stems removed (you can also use a half pound of asparagus, woody stalks removed)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon olive oil
- [ ] ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon garlic powder optional

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
2. Toss peas or asparagus with olive oil, thyme, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until coated.
3. Spread on a cookie sheet and bake for 8–12 minutes, until softened and just starting to brown. Serve warm.
