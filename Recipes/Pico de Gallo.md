## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 1/2 pounds ripe red tomatoes (about 4 medium), enough for 2 cups finely diced
- [ ] ½ cup minced white onion (about 1/4 large onion)
- [ ] 1 to 2 jalapeño peppers, seeds removed (depending on your spice tolerance)
- [ ] ¼ cup packed finely chopped cilantro
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon cumin
- [ ] 2 tablespoons lime juice
- [ ] ½ teaspoon [[16 Types Of Salt And How To Use Them#Kosher Salt|kosher salt]]

## Instructions

1. Dice the tomatoes, removing the core and seeds. It will take a while for this part, but hang in there!
2. Finely chop the onion. Remove the seeds from the jalapeño peppers and finely chop them. Finely chop the cilantro.
3. Mix the tomatoes, onion, jalapeño peppers, cilantro, cumin, lime juice, and [[16 Types Of Salt And How To Use Them#Kosher Salt|kosher salt]] in a bowl. Serve immediately. You can store a few days refrigerated as well. The pico de gallo will accumulate water as it sits; drain as necessary.

## Notes

- Using 1 seeded jalapeño pepper comes out very mild. If you’d like more spice, add the second pepper, and you can add some seeds as well to come to your desired spice level.
