## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 can (120 g drained weight) light flaked tuna in water, drained
- [ ] 15 mL (1 tablespoon) mayonnaise
- [ ] 5 mL (1 teaspoon) yellow mustard
- [ ] 5 mL (1 teaspoon) lemon juice
- [ ] 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
- [ ] 60 mL (¼ cup) green pepper, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 mL (½ teaspoons) black pepper
- [ ] 2 leaves romaine lettuce, washed and dried
- [ ] 2 whole grain [[Whole Wheat Flour Tortillas|tortillas]]

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, use a fork to combine tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, and lemon.
2. Add celery, green pepper and black pepper to the tuna mixture and mix until well distributed.
3. Place 1 lettuce leaf on each tortilla.
4. Scoop half of the tuna mixture onto each wrap and roll up.

## Notes

- Spice up your tuna salad by adding 2 mL (½ tsp) of curry powder.
- No wraps? No problem. Use whole grain bread to make a sandwich or serve with whole grain crackers.
