This easy 3-ingredient bread recipe proves you don’t need a long ingredient list to make real homemade bread. With yeast doing the work, this loaf comes out soft, simple, and perfect for beginners or anyone who wants fresh bread without overcomplicating it.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 ⅙ Cups (500g) All Purpose Flour
- [ ] 1 ⅔ Cups (400ml/400g) (80% Hydration) Warm Water (80 °F (ca. 27 °C) — 90 °F (ca. 32 °C))
- [ ] 1 ½ teaspoons (5g) Instant Yeast (or Dry Active Yeast)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5g) Kosher Salt

## Instructions

1. Combine dry ingredients, mix until combined
2. Add wet ingredients to wet and mix until combined and forms a ball
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and set aside for 4–6 hours
4. Remove to a lightly floured surface and gently form into a loaf and place in the Dutch Oven. Try not to rupture too many of the bubbles.
5. Place a bowl over the top of the loaf whilst heat oven and Dutch Oven to 450 °F (ca. 232 °C)
6. Place loaf into Dutch oven and lightly sprinkle with flour. Using kitchen shears make 4–5 snips down each side of the bread.
7. Cook 30-min covered
8. Cook an additional 15-min uncovered
9. Remove from oven and let rest on a wire rack for 35–45 minutes.
