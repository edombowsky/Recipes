## Ingredients

### French Onion Dip Mix

- [ ] 3 tablespoons (45 mL) onion powder
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 mL) garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 mL) golden granulated sugar crystals or packed golden brown sugar
- [ ] 1½ teaspoons (7 mL) salt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) fennel seed
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) freshly ground pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) thyme, crumbled

### Dill Pickle Dip Mix

- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 mL) dill weed
- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 mL) mixed pickling spice
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 mL) onion powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) garlic powder
- [ ] ½ teaspoon (2.5 mL) freshly ground pepper
- [ ] ½ teaspoon (2.5 mL) salt

### Spicy Chipotle Dip Mix

- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 mL) golden granulated sugar crystals or packed golden brown sugar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 mL) ground chipotle chilli pepper
- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 mL) paprika
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 mL) garlic powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) caraway seed
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 mL) salt

## Instructions

### Make the Dip Mix

1. Grind all ingredients in a spice mill or coffee grinder.
2. Alternatively, all ingredients may be ground using a mortar and pestle.

### To Make the Dip

1. 3/4 cup (170 ml) sour cream
2. 3 tablespoons (45 mL) mayonnaise
3. 2 tablespoons (30 mL) Mix
4. Add 1 tablespoon (15 mL) water until blended.
5. May be refrigerated for up to 3 days.
6. Makes about 1 cup (250 mL).

## Notes

- French Onion Dip Mix makes about 1/3 cup (75 mL)
- Dill Pickle Dip Mix makes about 1/4 cup (125 mL)
- Spicy Chipotle Dip Mix makes about 1/2 cup (125 mL)
