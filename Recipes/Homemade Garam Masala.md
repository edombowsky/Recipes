## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tablespoon cumin
- [ ] 1 tablespoon ground coriander
- [ ] 2 teaspoons cardamom
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- [ ] 1 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] ½ teaspoon ground cloves
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Combine all spices. Taste and adjust to suit your preference. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.

## Notes

For a more aromatic garam masala, add all spices to a sauté pan and heat for just a few minutes, until fragrant.
