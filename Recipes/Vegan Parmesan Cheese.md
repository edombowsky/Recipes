## Ingredients

- [ ] ½ cup raw cashews
- [ ] ¼ cup nutritional yeast flakes
- [ ] ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] ½ teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] ½ teaspoon white pepper (optional)
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients into the bowl of a food processor fitted with an s-blade.
2. Blend for a few seconds, until the mixture resembles Parmesan cheese.
3. Taste-test and adjust any seasoning to your liking.
4. Serve immediately or store in sealed container in the refrigerator or freezer.
