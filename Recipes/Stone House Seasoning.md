Stone House Seasoning Recipe – A quick, easy and delicious seasoning blend that adds so much flavour in a snap! Made of three simple ingredients, it really is one of those seasoning blends that combines the flavours that perk up so many dishes from meats to vegetables with such ease!

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/4 cup kosher salt
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
- [ ] 2 teaspoons granulated garlic, or garlic powder (1/2 the amount of granulated garlic)

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine the salt, pepper, and granulated garlic. The blend will keep in an airtight container for at least 1 year.
2. Use with beef, chicken, poultry, pork, fish, and vegetables.

## Notes

This recipe is easily modified to make less or more depending on your needs.
