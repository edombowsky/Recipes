## Ingredients

- [ ] 800 g / 28 oz canned tomato, crushed or diced
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon mustard powder
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon cooking/kosher salt
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix all the ingredients in a medium saucepan.
2. Simmer Bring to a simmer over medium heat then lower heat to medium low so it's simmering gently.
3. Cook for 25 minutes, stirring regularly, until it reduces down and is thick enough so you can draw a path across the base of the saucepan (see [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IhmIuBy0MrA)).
4. Transfer into a bowl. For use on burgers, cool then slather.

## Notes

**Substitutions** (it’s a forgiving recipe):

- Brown sugar ⇾ white sugar
- Garlic powder ⇾ 1/2 garlic clove finely minced or more onion powder
- Onion powder ⇾ more garlic powder
- Smoked paprika ⇾ ordinary paprika
- Mustard powder ⇾ 1/2 teaspoon any smooth mustard (perhaps not hot English mustard though!)
- Celery salt ⇾ ordinary salt

**Leftovers** will keep for 6 weeks in sterilized jars, a week in the fridge in ordinary containers, or 3 months in the freezer.
