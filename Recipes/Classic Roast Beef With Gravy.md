## Ingredients

### Roast

- [ ] 2 lbs (1kg) beef sirloin tip oven roast
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, finely grated or pressed
- [ ] 4 teaspoons minced fresh thyme
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoons salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon pepper

### Gravy

- [ ] 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- [ ] 1/3 cup dry red wine
- [ ] 2 cups sodium-reduced beef broth
- [ ] 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
- [ ] 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard

## Instructions

### Roast

1. Rub beef all over with oil, garlic, thyme, salt and half of the pepper. Place on greased rack in roasting pan.
2. Roast in 450 °F oven until beginning to brown, about 19 minutes.
3. Reduce heat to 275 °F (ca. 135 degree Celsius) and roast, basting occasionally, until instant-read thermometer inserted in centre reads 140 °F for medium-rare, about 1 hour.
4. Transfer to cutting board; tent with foil. Let rest for 15 minutes before thinly slicing across the grain.

### Gravy

1. While roast is resting, drain all but 2 tablespoons fat from the pan.
2. Sprinkle in flour; cook over medium-high heat, stirring, until golden, about 3 minutes.
3. Stir in wine, scraping up browned bits with a wooden spoon.
4. Whisk in broth and remaining pepper; bring to boil over medium heat.
5. Cook, whisking occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
6. Strain through fine-mesh sieve; stir in parsley, mustard and any accumulated juices from cutting board. Serve with beef.
