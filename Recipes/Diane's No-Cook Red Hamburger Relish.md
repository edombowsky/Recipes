## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup sweet green pickle relish
- [ ] 1 (5.5 ounce (ca. 208 g)) can tomato paste
- [ ] 2 tablespoons jarred roasted red peppers with garlic, finely chopped
- [ ] 1 teaspoon white vinegar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon white sugar
- [ ] 6 drops liquid smoke flavouring
- [ ] 1 pinch ground black pepper
- [ ] 1 dash ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix relish, tomato paste, roasted red peppers, vinegar, sugar, liquid smoke, black pepper, and cinnamon together in a bowl. Spoon mixture into a clean glass jar and screw the lid on tightly. Store in the refrigerator.

## Notes

- Use 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika instead of the liquid smoke and add another 2 tablespoons of the green relish.
