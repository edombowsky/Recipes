## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1-2 tablespoons chipotle chillies in adobo sauce, depending on how spicy you like it
- [ ] 1-1/2 cups tomato sauce
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon chipotle chilli powder
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- [ ] 3/4 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
- [ ] kosher salt and fresh pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Spray a medium pot with oil and add garlic, sauté 30 seconds. Add chipotle chillies, chilli powder, cumin, chicken broth, tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5–10 minutes, to let the flavours meld.
