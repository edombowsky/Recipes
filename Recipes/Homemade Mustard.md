## Ingredients

- [ ] ¾ cup yellow mustard seeds or ½ cup ground mustard powder
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sugar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon sweet paprika
- [ ] ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] 1 ½ cups water
- [ ] ¼ cup white vinegar

## Instructions

1. Grind seeds in a food processor or spice grinder to a very fine powder. Add mustard and other spices to a small sauce pot, whisk to combine, then add water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 30–40 minutes, until thickened, stirring occasionally. Add vinegar and cook for 5-10 more minutes, stirring often. Taste and adjust seasoning.
