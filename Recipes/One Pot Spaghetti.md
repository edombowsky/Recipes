## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tablespoon oil
- [ ] 1 lb (ca. 454 g) lean minced beef
- [ ] 1 cup chopped onions
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- [ ] 4 cups chicken broth
- [ ] 1 can (5 1/2 oz) tomato paste
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- [ ] 2 cups broken spaghetti
- [ ] freshly grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat, add beef, onion, and garlic, Cook stirring until browned. Drain off excess fat.
2. Stir in next six ingredients (broth through pepper). Bring to a boil and then add spaghetti. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring frequently, about 12–15 minutes.
3. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
