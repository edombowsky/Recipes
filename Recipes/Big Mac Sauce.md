## Ingredients

- [ ] ½ cup (110 grams) mayonnaise see notes for Greek yogurt option\*
- [ ] 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- [ ] 1 tablespoons minced onion I recommend yellow onion
- [ ] 2 teaspoons yellow mustard
- [ ] ½ teaspoon white vine vinegar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon granulated sugar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon paprika
- [ ] ½ teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

### Optional Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons Fresh dressing replace with ketchup or barbecue sauce

## Instructions

1. Mince the onion very finely.
2. Combine all ingredients in a medium-size bowl and give a good stir, until you get a creamy sauce.
3. Refrigerate homemade Big Mac sauce for at least 2 hours or overnight before serving.

## Notes

**How to make Big Mac Sauce with Greek yogurt**

For a low fat and low calorie Big Mac sauce, prepare this recipe with a combination of mayonnaise and Greek yogurt. My recommendation is using half mayonnaise (¼ cup) and half Greek yogurt (¼ cup).
