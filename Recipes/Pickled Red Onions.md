## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 medium red onion, halved and thinly sliced (about 2 cups)
- [ ] 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds, lightly crushed
- [ ] Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Put the onions in a medium bowl.
2. Combine the vinegar, sugar, coriander, 2 teaspoons salt and a few grinds of pepper in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
3. Cook until the sugar dissolves, about 1 minute.
4. Pour the mixture over the onions, cover and set aside until cooled to room temperature.

- [ ] 1 large red onion peeled and sliced very thin
- [ ] ¾ cup apple cider vinegar
- [ ] ¼ cup water
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1-2 Tablespoon sweetener of your choice

1. In a small saucepan, heat all the ingredients except the onion. Cook over medium heat and bring to a simmer.
2. Slice the onion very thinly and place in a mason jar
3. Pour the heated mixture into the jar to evenly cover the onion. Put the lid on and shake well
4. Marinate the onions for about 30 minutes
5. Serve immediately.
6. Onions can be kept in the jar for up to two weeks
