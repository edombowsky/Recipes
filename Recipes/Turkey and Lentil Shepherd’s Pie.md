## Ingredients

- [ ] 5 large potatoes (about 1 kg/2 ¼ lb), washed and cubed
- [ ] 1 yellow onion, diced
- [ ] 450 g (1 pound) lean ground turkey
- [ ] 250 mL (1 cup) canned lentils, rinsed and drained
- [ ] 30 mL (2 tablespoons) Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 30 mL (2 tablespoons) canned tomato paste
- [ ] 500 mL (2 cups) frozen peas and carrots, thawed
- [ ] 30 mL (2 tablespoons) soft non-hydrogenated margarine
- [ ] 15 mL (1 tablespoon) 1% milk

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes in a large pot of water and bring to a boil. Cook until the potatoes are soft, about 10 minutes and then drain.
2. Lightly coat a non-stick pan with cooking spray or oil and heat over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook for about 5 minutes, until browned. Add ground turkey and cook until brown.
3. Add lentils, Worcestershire sauce and tomato paste to the ground turkey.
4. Pour ground turkey mixture into an 8 x 8 casserole dish and spread evenly across the bottom. Layer peas and carrots on top of turkey mixture.
5. Add margarine and milk to the potatoes. Mash with a potato masher or a hand blender. Layer mashed potatoes overtop peas and carrots and spread evenly.
6. Place in the oven on the lower rack and broil for 5 to 10 minutes until potatoes begin to brown. Remove from the oven and serve.

## Notes

- Lentils can be dried or canned. If you use dried lentils, follow cooking instructions on the package and add them cooked at step 3.
- Use any other fresh or frozen vegetable you like such as leeks or mushrooms. Make sure to chop vegetables into small pieces and cook until tender.
