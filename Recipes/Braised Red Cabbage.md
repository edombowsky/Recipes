## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/4 cup butter
- [ ] 1 onion, chopped
- [ ] 1 apple, cored and diced
- [ ] ~1/3 cup raisins~ ***yuck***
- [ ] 3 tablespoons packed brown sugar
- [ ] 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; cook onion and apple until softened, about 5 mins.
2. Add cabbage and toss to combine.
3. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes. Stir in raisins, sugar, vinegar, salt, caraway seeds, and pepper,
4. Cover and cook until cabbage is tender but not mushy, 10 to 12 minutes.
