## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 jumbo shells, cooked
- [ ] 9 ounces (ca. 340 g) minced beef, cooked
- [ ] 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- [ ] salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] 3/4 cup ricotta cheese
- [ ] 3/4 cup tomato sauce
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400° F.
2. Turn each shell inside-out and place one jumbo shell in the cup of a muffin tin.
3. Evenly distribute the minced beef between all shells and season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Evenly distribute the ricotta cheese between all the shells.
5. Evenly distribute the tomato sauce between all the shells.
6. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly on top of each shell.
7. Bake for 20 minutes or less.
