## Ingredients

- [ ] 4-6 bone-in chicken thighs
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups water
- [ ] 1 cup soy sauce
- [ ] 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- [ ] 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger slivered
- [ ] 5 garlic cloves, crushed
- [ ] 7 spring onions cut in half
- [ ] 1 tablespoon cornstarch + 2 tablespoons water made into a slurry
- [ ] Garnish with sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients except cornstarch and green onions in a large pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
2. Reduce to low and simmer, partly covered, turning occasionally, until chicken is tender, about 30 to 35 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let chicken sit for 20 minutes.
4. Remove chicken to a serving platter. Remove garlic and ginger and discard.
5. Bring sauce to a boil, skim off excess fat.
6. Slowly whisk in the cornstarch slurry
7. Whisk in cornstarch mixture and bring to a boil.
8. Remove from heat, add chicken, turn to coat, and serve chicken with sauce and sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
