Need an appetizer that looks impressive but won’t keep you in the kitchen for hours? These Caprese salad bites come together in minutes with juicy tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil—drizzled in high-quality olive oil. You’ll be a kitchen hero in no time.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 cherry tomatoes ripe
- [ ] 12 mini mozzarella balls drained
- [ ] 12 fresh basil leaves rinsed
- [ ] Salt to taste
- [ ] Black pepper to taste
- [ ] Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Ingredients**: Wash and dry the cherry tomatoes. Drain the mozzarella balls and rinse the basil leaves.
2. **Assemble the Bites**: Place a cherry tomato on your work surface, then top it with a basil leaf and a mozzarella ball. Secure them with a toothpick or mini skewer. Repeat for all 12 bites.
3. **Season and Serve**: Sprinkle each bite with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Drizzle lightly with extra virgin olive oil (optional). Place each bite in a small glass or on a serving plate for an elegant touch.
4. **Enjoy Immediately**: These bites are best served right away, so don’t waste any time—your guests will be impressed!

## Notes

🍅 **Tomato Selection is Everything**: Don’t even think about using sad, squishy tomatoes. Go for _fresh, ripe cherry tomatoes_. If they’re not bursting with flavour, just skip the recipe altogether. You deserve better.

🧀 **Cheese Upgrade**: Sure, mini mozzarella balls are classic, but if you’re feeling fancy (and like you just got back from a Tuscan holiday), go for _burrata_ instead. It’s basically mozzarella’s creamier, fancier cousin. Trust me, your taste buds will thank you.

🌱 **Basil, But Make It Fresh**: Dried basil? Don’t even. Fresh basil is where it’s at. Grab a handful from the grocery store, rinse it, and feel like an absolute culinary pro. No, it doesn’t count if it’s from a jar in your pantry.

🍴 **Get Fancy with the Skewers**: Toothpicks work fine, but if you want to _really_ impress, go for mini skewers. They make these little bites look like they belong on the cover of a food magazine. Plus, you get to feel like you’re hosting a fancy dinner party. You’re welcome.

🧂 **Season Like a Boss**: Salt and pepper are great, but don’t just sprinkle them like you’re afraid of flavour. Season generously! Just a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper goes a long way in bringing all those fresh ingredients to life.

🥄 **Olive Oil—Don’t Skimp**: Don’t even think about using regular olive oil. Extra virgin is the only way to go. If you’re using the cheap stuff, why bother making these at all? You’ll taste the difference, and trust me, your guests will notice.

🎯 **Make Ahead…Sort Of**: These bites are best served fresh, but you can _prep_ them ahead of time. Stick everything together (minus the oil), then drizzle the olive oil just before serving. Keep them in the fridge for up to 3 hours—any longer and the mozzarella will start to get weird.
