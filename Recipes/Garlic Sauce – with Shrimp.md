## Ingredients

### Sauce

- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon sambal
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1 cup snow peas
- 1 red holland or jalapeño pepper, diced small
- 1 pound large peeled and de-veined shrimp, 21/25’s would be perfect
- Kosher salt
- Diced green onion for garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine sauce ingredients in a small bow, mix well and set aside
2. Put a pan over medium high heat and add a tablespoon of oil
3. When almost smoking put in the snow peas and cook until crisp tender, 3-4 minutes then and add the diced pepper and cook another minute or 2
4. Add the shrimp, and cook stirring often until almost done, 2-3 minutes, then add the sauce and continue cooking until slightly thickened and the shrimp and veggies are beautifully coated - season to taste with salt
5. Garnish with green onions
