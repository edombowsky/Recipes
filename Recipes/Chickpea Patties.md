## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cans cooked chickpeas (480g | 16oz)
- [ ] 3 tablespoons chickpea “water”
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 3 large garlic cloves
- [ ] 10oz | 280g fresh spinach
- [ ] 6oz | 190g | 1 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried herbs
- [ ] 1 teaspoon red chilli flakes
- [ ] Season with salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375° F
2. Place chickpeas and 3 tablespoons of the water into a food processor.
3. Slice the onion and sauté for 3 minutes.
4. Mince tree large garlic cloves and cook with the onions for 2 minutes.
5. Add the spinach to the onion mixture and stir until it wilts down.
6. Add the spinach and onion mixture to the chickpeas in the food processor.
7. Add the bread crumbs, dried herbs, and chilli flakes to the food processor.
8. Season with salt to taste.
9. Blend the mixture until well combined.
10. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
11. Form into 2 oz (60g) patties and place on a lightly oiled baking tray.
12. Bake for 15 minutes.

## Notes

- Feel free to add your favourite dried herbs and spices to get the flavour you enjoy the most.
