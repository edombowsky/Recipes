## Ingredients

- [ ] 10 cups popped corn
- [ ] 1/4 cup melted unsalted butter
- [ ] 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon chilli powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cumin
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

Melt 1/4 cup unsalted butter in a saucepan. In a small bowl whisk together 1/2 cup nutritional yeast, 1 teaspoon chilli powder, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Drizzle butter and sprinkle nutritional yeast mixture over the popcorn, and stir until the popcorn is well-coated.
