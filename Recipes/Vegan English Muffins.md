## Ingredients

- [ ] 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
- [ ] 2 teaspoons sugar
- [ ] 2 teaspoons instant yeast
- [ ] 1 teaspoons baking powder
- [ ] a pinch of salt
- [ ] 2 tablespoons vegan butter or oil
- [ ] 3/4 cup + 2 tablespoons lukewarm water

## Instructions

1. Knead until it's a soft dough (takes about 5 mins).
2. Let it rest for 1 hour.
3. Shape into 8 balls.
4. Let them rest for 40 mins.
5. Flatten each ball.
6. Fry on medium heat 4-5mins each side fluffy.
