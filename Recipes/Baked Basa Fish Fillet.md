## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 basa fish fillets
- [ ] 4 teaspoons unsalted butter
- [ ] 1/2 tablespoons sweet paprika
- [ ] 1/2 tablespoon dried basil
- [ ] 1/8 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/8 teaspoon lemon juice
- [ ] 4 lemon slices

## Instructions

1. Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper. Put basa fish on the baking sheet.
2. Melt butter in the small glass bowl. Add sweet paprika, dried basil, salt, and lemon juice.
3. Spread the marinade on both sides of fish using cooking brush or spoon. Marinate for 20 minutes.
4. Put lemon slices on top of the fish.
5. Preheat oven to 350 °F (ca. 177 degree Celsius).
6. Bake for 30 minutes uncovered.

## Notes

- Basa Fish – white fish. Other white fish can be used for this recipe, for example: tilapia, cod, haddock. Just remember the baking time may vary.
- Unsalted Butter – salted butter can be substituted but use less salt in the recipe.
- Dried Basil – fresh basil can be used as well.
- Lemon Slices – to place on fish before baking. Lime can be used instead of lemon.
- Marinate the fish for about 20 minutes. I used a silicone basting brush to spread marinate nicely.
- Also, I used my favourite freeze-dried basil for the marinade, but you can use fresh if you have it, this will enhance the flavour.
- It is the best to put fish on parchment paper to prevent it from sticking to the pan.
- Meal Prep Tips: The cooked fish can be stored in glass or plastic container in the fridge.
- What to serve with basa fish? You can serve it with mashed potatoes, kale salad and baked zucchini fries. These fish fillets will taste great with any grains like quinoa, barley or buckwheat, sweet potato or carrot fries will pair nicely also.
