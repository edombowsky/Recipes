## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried savoury
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried marjoram
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried lavender or rosemary
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried thyme
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried basil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried tarragon
- [ ] _Optional_: 1 teaspoon dried bay leaf (_see notes_)

## Instructions

1. Add the dried spices to a bowl and mix them well.
2. Store in a container in a cool, dark area like your pantry for 6–12 months.

## Notes

- Use your fingers to break apart and crush a dried bay leaf into tiny pieces. Bay leaf has a very strong flavour so don't be tempted to add more than 1 teaspoon.
- Many of the herbs in this recipe are also found in Italian seasoning. Most of the time, you can easily substitute one for the other.
- Another recipe source: [What Are Herbs de Provence? How to Make (and Use) Your Own Provençal Herb Blend - 2024 - MasterClass](https://www.masterclass.com/articles/what-are-herbs-de-provence)
