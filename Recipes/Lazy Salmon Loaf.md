## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 can, 14 ounces (398 ml) salmon, drained and deboned
- [ ] 1 cup soft dried bread crumbs or 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs or 6 to 10 crackers crumbled
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- [ ] 1.2 teaspoon cooking onion
- [ ] 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] a few drops of Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C)
2. Combine salmon and bread crumbs or crackers in a small bowl
3. Add all other ingredients and mix well
4. Transfer the mixture to a casserole dish (or loaf pan)
5. Cover and bake for 30 minutes
6. Remove cover for last 10 minutes
