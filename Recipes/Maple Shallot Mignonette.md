After making [[How To Grill Pink And Spiny Scallops]] you can eat them plain or top them with your favourite sauce.  At the BC Seafood Festival, Chef Josh Gonneau served them with this delicious Maple Shallot Mignonette.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 125 mL Red Wine Vinegar
- [ ] 25 mL Good Maple Syrup
- [ ] 1⁄2 to 1 whole, small Shallot, diced
- [ ] 1 tsp Fresh dill, chopped
- [ ] Salt and Coarse Cracked Pepper To Taste (should be little peppery)

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients together and serve with grilled scallops.
