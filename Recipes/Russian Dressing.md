## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 3 tablespoons ketchup-style chilli sauce
- [ ] 1 tablespoon minced white onion
- [ ] 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more as needed
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more as needed

## Instructions

1. Place 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons ketchup-style chilli sauce, 1 tablespoon minced white onion, 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish, 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika, 1/2 kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a medium bowl and stir to combine. Taste and add more kosher salt or black pepper as needed.

## Notes

**Storage:** Russian dressing can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
