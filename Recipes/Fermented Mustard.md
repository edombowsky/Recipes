## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 quart (ca. 1,137 ml) mason jar
- [ ] 1 cup black mustard seeds
- [ ] 1 cup yellow mustard seeds
- [ ] 2 cups water
- [ ] 1 tablespoon salt
- [ ] 1/4 cup vinegar (sauerkraut juice, fermented pickle brine or Kombucha)

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients into the mason jar and cover lightly.
2. Place on counter for at least 1 week, stirring every day or two.
3. Drain mustard seeds and place in a mortar and pestle. Grind into a paste.
