## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch (1-cm) squares
- [ ] 1 onion, chopped
- [ ] 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- [ ] 3 tablespoons (45 ml) vegetable oil
- [ ] 1 lb (454 g) ground beef
- [ ] 1/4 cup (60 ml) chilli powder
- [ ] 2 teaspoons (10 ml) ground coriander
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 ml) ground cumin
- [ ] 1 teaspoon (5 ml) ground ginger
- [ ] 1 pinch cayenne (optional)
- [ ] 3 tablespoons (45 ml) molasses
- [ ] 3 tablespoons (45 ml) ketchup
- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 ml) Dijon mustard
- [ ] 2 tablespoons (30 ml) red wine vinegar
- [ ] 1 can (796 ml/28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- [ ] 1 can (540 ml/19 oz) red kidney beans
- [ ] Salt and pepper
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 ml) whole-grain mustard

## Instructions

1. In a Dutch oven or large pot over medium heat, soften the bell pepper, onion, and garlic in the oil.
2. Add the meat and brown.
3. Add the spices and cook for 2 minutes.
4. Add the molasses and ketchup and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add the Dijon mustard, vinegar, tomatoes, and beans. Lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Remove from the heat and stir in the whole-grain mustard.
