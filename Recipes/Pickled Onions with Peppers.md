## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 1/2 onions, julienned
- [ ] 2 cup of white vinegar
- [ ] 2 cups of water
- [ ] 1/2 cup of white sugar
- [ ] 3 cloves of garlic, sliced
- [ ] 1 tbsp of sea salt
- [ ] 1 tsp of Five Spice Powder (optional)
- [ ] 1/2 Green Bell Pepper, julienned (optional)

## Instructions

1. Add the white vinegar and water to a pot. Mix in the sugar and a spoonful of sea salt. This is the basic mixture, but optionally you can add five spice powder if you want to be fancy.
2. Bring this to a boil, as soon as it comes to a boil you add the onions peppers and garlic and stir and cook for 1 minute
3. Drain the liquid from the onions and peppers, saving the liquid.
4. Cool these both, and the quicker the cooling, the crunchier the pickles are going to stay. So putting the onion mixture on a sheet pan in the refrigerator is best.
5. Once you have everything cooled, you can put the onions in a jar, and use some of the pickling liquid that is now cold and top the jars up.
