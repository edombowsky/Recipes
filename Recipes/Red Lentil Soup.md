## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup red lentils
- [ ] 1 carrot
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 1 small potato
- [ ] 1 tablespoon butter
- [ ] 1 teaspoon powdered red pepper
- [ ] Salt

## Instructions

1. Wash and drain the lentils.
2. Add butter, chopped carrots, onion, and potato in a pot and sauté few minutes, then add drained lentils, sauté for additional minute, then add 6 cups of water and salt (you can use some chicken stock too if you want)
3. Boil on low heat until carrots soften and blend them after they are cooked.
4. If desired, melt the butter in a small pan, add powdered pepper and mix and pour over the soup.
