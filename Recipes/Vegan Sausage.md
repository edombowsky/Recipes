## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 ½ cups vital wheat gluten 180 g
- [ ] ½ cup [[Easy Homemade Vegetable Broth|vegetable broth]] 120 mL
- [ ] ¼ cup tomato paste 50 g
- [ ] ¼ cup soy sauce 60 mL
- [ ] 1 teaspoon each smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, coriander, black pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon liquid smoke 5 mL

## Instructions

1. **Dough**: In a large bowl, stir together all ingredients until a ball forms. Transfer to a clean surface and knead for about 2 minutes, or until dough is elastic and cohesive (it will still be lumpy, that's okay!)
2. **Shape**: Divide into 15 even pieces, then shape each piece into a log. Each log should be about ½ inch thick. Wrap each log tightly in parchment paper and aluminium foil, making sure the foil overlaps so it doesn't open during cooking, then twist the ends to secure.
3. **Steam**: Bring a pot of water with a steamer basket to a boil, then place wrapped sausages into the basket. Cover and let steam for 30 minutes. (Keep an eye on the water level to make sure water doesn't all evaporate.)
4. **Grill**: Remove plant based sausages from the basket and gently unwrap. Transfer to a well-oiled hot grill pan (or grill) and cook for about 5 minutes, turning often, until charred on the outside.

## Notes

- **Prepare the wrappings in advance** for quicker assembly! I like to cut large squares of aluminum foil, then place smaller squares of parchment onto each foil. Then just roll the sausages and twist to secure.
