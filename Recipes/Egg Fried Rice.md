## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 large eggs
- [ ] 1 cup (210g) cooked and cooled rice, preferably day-old
- [ ] 1 tbsp oyster sauce (or salt, soy sauce, concentrated chicken stock, chicken bouillon powder, to taste)
- [ ] 1 tbsp neutral-tasting oil
- [ ] 1-2 green onions

## Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Add the rice, oyster sauce, and cooking oil. Stir to combine.
2. Thinly slice the green onions: white parts to stir-fry and green parts to garnish.
3. In a wok (or pan), heat the oil (1 tbsp) over **medium-high heat**. Once it gets nice and hot, add the white parts of the green onions. Saute for **1 minute** or until fragrant.
4. Increase the heat to **high**. Add the rice and the egg mixture. Using a spatula, keep stirring it until most of the liquid has evaporated and every single grain is completely coated.
5. Add the green parts of the green onions. Stir-fry for **another** **1-2 minutes**. Place on a serving plate. Enjoy!
