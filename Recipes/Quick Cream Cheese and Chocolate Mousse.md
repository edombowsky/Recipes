## Ingredients

- [ ] 200g of white chocolate
- [ ] ~ 1 cup of cream cheese (200g)
- [ ] ~ 1 and 3/4 cup of whipping cream (400ml)

## Instructions

1. Grate the chocolate
2. Melt in a bain-marie or in the microwave
3. Remove from heat and mix in ~ 1 cup of cream cheese
4. Beat with a mixer until you obtain a smooth mixture without lumps
5. Chill for 10-15 minutes
6. In another bowl beat 1 3/4 cup of whipping cream until it forms stiff peaks
7. Gradually fold into the chocolate mixture
8. Pipe into small ramekins and chill for 4-5 hours or overnight
9. Decorate with some grated white chocolate and mint leaves (optional)
