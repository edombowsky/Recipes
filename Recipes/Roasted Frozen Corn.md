## Ingredients

- [ ] 24 oz frozen corn
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- [ ] salt
- [ ] pepper
- [ ] olive oil spray

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
2. Spray a baking sheet with olive oil spray.
3. Place frozen corn on the oiled baking sheet
4. Spray the top of the corn with olive oil spray.
5. Sprinkle on even amounts of salt and pepper. If you are unsure of how much to use, start with a teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of pepper. You can always add more to taste after the corn is done roasting.
6. Sprinkle the garlic powder on top of the corn evenly.
7. Roast at 425 degrees for 10 minutes
8. Remove corn and stir it around, then sprinkle on the grated Parmesan cheese.
9. Roast 15 more minutes, then stir again.
10. Roast for an additional 5–15 minutes, until corn is golden brown on the edges.

## Notes

The total baking time will vary based on the type of baking sheet you use. I personally love crispier, chewier corn, so I roasted mine on the longer side.
