## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cups chopped cucumbers (unpeeled)
- [ ] 1 cup each chopped sweet red pepper yellow and green peppers
- [ ] 1 cup chopped celery
- [ ] 1 cup chopped onion
- [ ] 1/4 cup pickling salt
- [ ] 4 cups chopped seeded peeled tomatoes
- [ ] 3 cups white vinegar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
- [ ] 1 teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon allspice
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- [ ] 2 cups granulated sugar
- [ ] 1/4 cup tomato paste

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, combine cucumbers, sweet peppers, celery, onion, and salt; cover with boiling water and let stand for 1 hour. Drain; rinse under cold water and drain well.
2. In large heavy saucepan, bring to boil tomatoes, vinegar, mustard seeds, turmeric, cinnamon, allspice and cayenne pepper, stirring often; reduce heat to medium and simmer for 30 minutes or until tomatoes are soft. Stir in sugar and drained vegetables; bring to boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat to medium and boil gently, stirring frequently, until thickened, about 20 minutes. Stir in tomato paste about 5 minutes before end of cooking time.
3. Fill and seal jars; process in boiling water bath for 10 minutes.
