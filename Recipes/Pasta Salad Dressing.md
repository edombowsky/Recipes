## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 clove garlic
- [ ] 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
- [ ] 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- [ ] 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- [ ] 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

Finely grate or mince 1 garlic clove and place in a mason jar. Add 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/2 cup red wine vinegar, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons dried oregano, 1 teaspoon kosher salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper. Seal the jar and shake well to combine and emulsify. (Alternatively, place all the ingredients in a small bowl and whisk well to combine.)

## Notes

**Make ahead:** The dressing can be made up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated in an airtight container.

**Storage:** Leftovers can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
