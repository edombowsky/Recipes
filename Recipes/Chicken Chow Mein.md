## Ingredients

### Sauce

- [ ] 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- [ ] 1/2 crushed bullion cube
- [ ] 2 tablespoons water
- [ ] 2 tablespoons toasted seasame oil
- [ ] 2 teaspoons cornstartch
- [ ] 2 teaspoons sugar, or maple syrup or agave syrup
- [ ] 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Sriracha hot sauce

### Main Dish

- [ ] 1 onion sliced
- [ ] 2 gloves garlic minced
- [ ] 2 carrots julienned
- [ ] 1 cup napa cabbage thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 head of bok choy thinly sliced, separate whites from greens
- [ ] cooked chicken shredded
- [ ] sesame seeds
- [ ] green onions

## Instructions

1. Sauté onions and garlic until garlic becomes golden.
2. Add vegetables (without green tops of bok choy) and cook until softened, but carrots still have a crunch.
3. While vegetables are cooking prepare Chow Mein noddles according to package instructions.
4. Once noodles are cooked drain and rice with cold water to stop cooking process.
5. Once vegetables are ready add chicken back to pan along with the green bok choy leaves.
6. Stir until well mixed and heated through.
7. Add on noodles and cook for another 45 seconds to a minute or so.
8. Add sauce and continue to heat for another one to two minutes to allow it to thicken up.
9. Garnish with toasted seasame seeds and sliced green onions
