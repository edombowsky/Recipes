## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 large (or 8 small) lamb chops (about 1-1/2 lbs/750 g)
- [ ] Salt and pepper
- [ ] 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- [ ] 1 onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1/2 cup finely chopped green or red pepper (optional)
- [ ] 1 cup uncooked white rice
- [ ] pinch each oregano, basil, and saffron
- [ ] 1-1/2 cups chicken stock
- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes, fresh or canned
- [ ] chopped fresh basil or parsley

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle chops with salt and pepper. In frying-pan, heat oil and brown chops on both sides, remove and set aside.
2. Add onion, garlic, and green pepper (if using) to frying-pan; cook until softened but not browned. Add rice, oregano, basil, and saffron; stir until rice is coloured. Stir in chicken stock, tomatoes, a few grindings of pepper, and a little salt (may not be needed if stock is salty).
3. Return browned chops to frying-pan along with any accumulated juices. Bring to boil; reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 30 minutes or until liquid is absorbed. Sprinkle with chopped basil before serving.
