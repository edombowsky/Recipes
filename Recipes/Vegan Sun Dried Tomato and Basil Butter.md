## Ingredients

- [ ] 8 oz butter or vegan butter
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chopped sun dried tomatoes
- [ ] 2 tablespoons basil chiffonade
- [ ] ½ teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Leave butter at room temperature. Once soft mash with a fork in a bowl.
2. Add the rest of your ingredients and mix until combined.
3. Form a log with your butter and wrap in plastic wrap, tying the ends.
4. Let sit in fridge 20 minutes to solidify and slice and serve! Goes great on butter, steaks, vegetables, etc.
