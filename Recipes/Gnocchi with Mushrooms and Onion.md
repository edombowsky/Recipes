## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 package (16 ounces (ca. 605 g)) potato gnocchi
- [ ] 1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
- [ ] 3/4 cup chopped sweet onion
- [ ] 1/4 cup butter, cubed
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- [ ] Grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook gnocchi according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large cast-iron frying-pan, sauté mushrooms and onion in butter until tender.
2. Drain gnocchi. Add the gnocchi, salt, Italian seasoning and pepper flakes to the frying-pan; heat through. Sprinkle with cheese.

## Nutrition

3/4 cup: 287 calories, 11g **fat** (6g **saturated fat**), 31 mg **cholesterol**, 583 mg **sodium**, 41g **carbohydrate** (7g **sugars**, 3g **fibre**), 8g **protein**.
