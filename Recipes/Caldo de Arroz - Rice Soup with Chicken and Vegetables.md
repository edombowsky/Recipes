## Ingredients

### Broth

- [ ] 1 large Chicken Breast (skinless with bone)
- [ ] 2 Chicken Thighs (with skin and bones)
- [ ] 1/2 onion
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Salt (or you decide)
- [ ] 10 C. hot water

### Soup

- [ ] 1 C Jasmine Rice
- [ ] 3 tablespoons Canola oil
- [ ] 3 medium carrots (small pieces)
- [ ] 3 small / medium Celery ribs (small pieces)
- [ ] corn from 2 cobs (you can use frozen or canned)
- [ ] 1 cup chopped onions
- [ ] 1/4 C. Chopped green onions
- [ ] 3 small Calabacitas (or zucchini)
- [ ] 3 Roma tomatoes
- [ ] 2 Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (1 tablespoon) (optional)
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon whole Cumin (or ground)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns (or ground)
- [ ] 2 large garlic cloves
- [ ] 2 teaspoon Tomato paste
- [ ] 10 cups chicken broth (the one used to boil chicken)
- [ ] small bunch Cilantro Chopped
- [ ] 2 cups water for blender
- [ ] 1/2 cup water for Molcajete

## Instructions

1. Add a tablespoon of oil to a saucepan and sear the chicken for about 3 minutes on one side.
2. After turning, add half of a medium onion and cook for another 3 minutes.
3. Add the 10 cups of water and the one tablespoon of salt.
4. Bring this to a boil then turn down to a medium-low, cover and simmer for 30 to 35 minutes. That is, until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165º F
5. If using whole spices, add one teaspoon of whole peppercorns 3/4 teaspoon of whole cumin. Once they are nicely ground, use 1/2 cup of water to bring them all together.
6. Once the chicken has cooked, set it aside and let it cool down for about 10 minutes.
7. Cut up tomatoes and place in a blender and give them a couple of pulses. Then add the chipotle peppers and Adobo sauce. Make sure the lid is tight and blend it really well.
8. Put a Dutch Oven on a medium heat and add 3 tablespoons of Canola oil. Add the onions and scallions. Then add the rest of the vegetables and let them seat for a few minutes (15 minutes or so). Now add the tomato paste and corn. The add the rice and combine all well. Add the mixture from the blender. After passing the chicken broth through a sieve add it to the Dutch oven.
9. Bring the oven to a boil. Once boiling, bring down the temperature to a medium low and cover and simmer.
10. Now shred the chicken from the bones and add to the soup once the rice is almost cooked, in about 25 minutes.
