## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- [ ] ½ cup white vinegar
- [ ] ¼ cup water
- [ ] 3 tablespoons sugar
- [ ] ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a small sauce pot over medium heat and whisk to combine. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, until reduced and thickened. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
