## Ingredients

- [ ] ¾ cup buttermilk
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
- [ ] 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
- [ ] Freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk buttermilk, mustard, dill, chives and vinegar in a jug to combine. Season with pepper.

### Tips

- Add 1 teaspoon Tabasco sauce for a touch of heat.
- For a thicker consistency, replace buttermilk with plain Greek yoghurt.
- Use different herbs to vary the flavour. Try basil or parsley.
- Delicious served with grilled chicken and a leafy salad.
