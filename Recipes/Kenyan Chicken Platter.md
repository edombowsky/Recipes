## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- [ ] 2 lb (ca. 907 g) Chicken thighs, skinless and boneless, cut into bite size
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon thyme, dry
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- [ ] Pinch red pepper flakes (optional)
- [ ] 1 yellow onion, peeled and sliced medium size
- [ ] 3 Yukon gold, peeled and cut into small bite size cubes
- [ ] 1 tablespoon garlic, chopped
- [ ] 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped for cooking and garnishing

## Instructions

1. Add oil to a hot frying pan. Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Add chicken to frying pan to brown it on all sides.
2. To the pan add the seasoning of cumin seeds, fennel seeds, dry thyme, paprika, and red pepper flakes. Continue to sauté the chicken until the seasoning becomes fragrant.
3. Add the onions and cook until it starts to soften.
4. Remove the chicken and onion mixture with a slotted spoon and set aside for now.
5. Add the potatoes and possibly a touch more oil the then pan. Season with a bit of salt and pepper. Stir until the salt and pepper covers the potatoes, then cover with a lid and turn down to a low heat.
6. Cook the potatoes for about 1200 minutes until they are softened and browned, stirring occasionally.
7. Add in the garlic and sauté for a few minutes and then add some cilantro and cook for a further minute or so.
8. Add back the onion and potato mixture and cook until everything is cooked through.
9. Lower the heat and add a squeeze of lemon juice and the remaining cilantro.
