## Ingredients

- [ ] 5 ripe mangos peeled and cut into ½ inch pieces
- [ ] 1 stick cinnamon about 2 inches (ca. 5 cm)
- [ ] 4 whole cloves
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon coriander seeds
- [ ] 6 cardamom pods cracked
- [ ] 2 cups of water
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ginger paste
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic paste
- [ ] 1/2 cup vinegar
- [ ] 1 whole dried red chilli optional
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 2 cups brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Add mango, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, coriander, cardamom, water, ginger, and garlic to a large pot. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes. Add in the vinegar, dried red chillies, salt, and sugar. Bring to a boil then simmer for an additional 30–40 minutes, until the chutney is thickened.
2. Using a small spoon, remove the cinnamon stick, cardamom, and cloves. Transfer the mango chutney into a jar while it’s still hot. Let cool and keep in the refrigerator for up to 4 weeks.
