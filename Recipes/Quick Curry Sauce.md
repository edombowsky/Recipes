## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups white onion coarsely chopped
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic minced
- [ ] 1 tablespoon fresh ginger grated
- [ ] 3 tablespoons curry powder
- [ ] 2 teaspoons sugar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cayenne powder optional
- [ ] 1 teaspoon coarse kosher salt
- [ ] 3 tablespoons tomato paste
- [ ] 1 cup low sodium chicken or vegetable stock
- [ ] 1 cup light coconut milk

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the onions, allowing to soften, approximately 5 minutes.
2. Add the garlic and ginger, sautéing until soft.
3. Stir in the curry powder, cayenne, salt, and tomato paste. Heat until fragrant.
4. Add the stock and coconut milk. Using a stick blender, blend until smooth. Alternately, you can transfer to a food processor or blender.
5. Use however you’d like to save for later.
