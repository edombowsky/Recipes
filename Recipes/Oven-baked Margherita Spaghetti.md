## Ingredients

- [ ] 125g wholemeal spaghetti
- [ ] 2 tablespoons dried red lentils
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, crushed
- [ ] 1 small onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- [ ] 2 teaspoons olive oil
- [ ] 400g can no-added salt cherry tomatoes
- [ ] ½ cup grated pizza cheese blend
- [ ] ¼ cup fresh basil leaves

## Instructions

1. Break spaghetti in half. Arrange over the base of an ovenproof dish (2L capacity).
2. Sprinkle lentils, garlic, onion and oregano over spaghetti. Pour over oil, tomatoes and 1½ cups hot water. Season with pepper. Stir well to combine.
3. Cover dish with foil and bake in a 200°C preheated oven for 30 minutes, stirring halfway through Cooking_Time.
4. Remove dish from oven, uncover and stir well. Sprinkle with cheese. Return to oven. Bake uncovered for a further 10 minutes or until spaghetti is tender, cheese is melted and top is lightly browned.
5. Sprinkle with fresh basil leaves, to serve.

### Tips

- Some canned tomatoes contain salt so always check the label to ensure no salt has been added. If canned cherry tomatoes are unavailable used 400g can no added salt diced tomatoes
- Use ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley instead of basil, if preferred.
