## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tablespoon canola oil
- [ ] 1 brown onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 1 tablespoon korma curry paste
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- [ ] 250g no added salt chickpeas, rinsed, drained
- [ ] 250g no added salt cannellini beans, rinsed, drained
- [ ] 2 eggs, lightly beaten
- [ ] 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 tablespoons wholemeal plain flour
- [ ] Spray olive oil
- [ ] 60g spinach leaves
- [ ] 2 tomatoes, sliced
- [ ] 1/2 cup carrot, grated
- [ ] 4 multigrain rolls

## Instructions

1. Heat non-stick frying pan over medium heat and drizzle with canola oil. Cook onion for 2 minutes, stir in curry paste and cumin and cook for 1 minute. Remove from the heat.
2. Mash chickpeas and cannellini beans in a large bowl. Add onion mix, egg, coriander and flour.
3. Shape mixture into 4 large patties. Spray a large non-stick frying pan with spray olive oil and set over medium heat. Add patties, turning once for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
4. Place spinach, tomato, grated carrot on each roll base and top with pattie and roll lid to serve.
