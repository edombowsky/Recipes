## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/4 cup ground black pepper
- [ ] 1/4 cup ground allspice
- [ ] 1/4 cup garlic powder
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground cloves
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground nutmeg
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground cardamom
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chilli powder
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- [ ] 1 tablespoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl or ziplock bag. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
2. **To marinate the shawarma meat:** You'll need 2 tablespoons olive oil + 1 tablespoon white vinegar + 1/2 teaspoon salt. Allow chicken to marinate for 10–20 minutes, and 1 hour to 24 hours for beef. Here's a recipe for [[Beef Shawarma Bowls with Persian Rice]] we love to make with this seasoning.
