## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tablespoon olive oil 15 mL
- [ ] 1 white onion chopped, about 1 cup
- [ ] 4 cloves garlic minced
- [ ] 2 tablespoons fresh grated ginger
- [ ] 1 tablespoons [[Homemade Garam Masala|garam masala]]
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 2 15-oz cans chickpeas drained, 425 g cans
- [ ] 2 15-oz cans tomato purée 425 g cans
- [ ] 1 14-oz can full-fat coconut milk 400 g
- [ ] Serve with: rice, sliced [[Naan Bread|naan]], cilantro

## Instructions

1. **Sauté**: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, cooking until soft and fragrant, about 3 minutes. Add seasoning and continue cooking for 2 minutes.
2. **Combine**: Add chickpeas, tomato purée, and coconut milk. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until hot and simmering.
3. **Serve**: Serve hot over a mound of rice with sliced [[Naan Bread|naan]], optionally garnishing with fresh cilantro.
