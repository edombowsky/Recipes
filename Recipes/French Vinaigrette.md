## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 medium shallot minced
- [ ] 1/2 cup champagne vinegar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard grainy or fine
- [ ] 2 1/2 tablespoons honey plus more to taste
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon [Piment d'Espelette](https://www.delightedcooking.com/what-is-piment-despelette.htm) optional
- [ ] 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- [ ] 1/4 cup sunflower oil or other neutral-flavoured oil, such as avocado oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chives minced
- [ ] 1 tablespoon tarragon minced
- [ ] 1 tablespoon flat leaf parsley minced
- [ ] salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Add the minced shallot and champagne vinegar to a bowl or large mason jar and allow the mixture to sit for 5 minutes.
2. Add the Dijon mustard, honey, [Piment d'Espelette](https://www.delightedcooking.com/what-is-piment-despelette.htm) (if using), the olive oil and avocado oil and whisk until combined.
3. Add the minced chives, tarragon, and parsley, and mix once more.
4. Taste test and add salt and pepper to taste, then serve with your favourite salad greens and enjoy!

## Notes

- Piment d’Espelette adds colour and flavour, but is optional.
- You can use apple cider vinegar or sherry vinegar in place of champagne vinegar.
- French vinaigrette can be saved in the fridge for up to 5 days.
