## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 can (540 mL/19 oz) lentils, drained and rinsed
- [ ] 60 mL (¼ cup) rolled oats
- [ ] 30 mL (2 tablespoons) lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
- [ ] 10 mL (2 teaspoons) dried basil
- [ ] 10 mL (2 teaspoons) cumin
- [ ] 5 mL (1 teaspoon) garlic powder
- [ ] 15 mL (1 tablespoon) olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a blender or food processor, place all ingredients (except for olive oil) and blend until smooth.
2. Form mini patties with your hands by rolling about 15 mL (1 tablespoon) of the mixture and then moulding into the shape of little disks (or any other shape you like). Place patties on a plate.
3. In a large frying-pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Gently transfer the nuggets to the frying-pan and pan-fry for about 2 to 3 minutes per side or until golden brown on both sides. Remove from frying-pan and allow to cool.

## Notes

- If the mixture is too moist, add extra oats (about 2 tbsp at a time). If the mixture is too sticky, use an ice cream scoop instead of your hands.
- Want an added kick? Try adding paprika or cayenne pepper powder at step 1.
