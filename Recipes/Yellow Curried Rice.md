## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup basmati rice
- [ ] 1.5 cups chicken stock
- [ ] 1 tablespoon ghee
- [ ] 1/4 inch ginger, minced
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 2 teaspoon curry powder
- [ ] 1 cinnamon stick
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350° F. Add the basmati rice to a fine mesh strainer and rinse underwater until the water runs clear. Set aside.
2. In a pot separate from the pot you will be cooking your rice in, heat up the stock until it is simmering and turn off the heat.
3. While the stock is heating up, ion an oven safe pot over medium heat, add ghee. Sauté the ginger and garlic for 1–2 minutes until fragrant. Add the curry powder and cinnamon stick and toast another 1–2 minutes until fragrant. Add the rice and stir it until it is yellow and coated.
4. Stir in the hot chicken stock and bring it up to a simmer. Top the pot with a lid and transfer to the oven for 18 minutes. After 18 minutes, remove it from the oven and let it sit covered on the worktop for 3 minutes.
5. After the three minutes remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork. Serve and enjoy!

## Notes

- If you do not like ghee you can use whole butter, olive oil, or fat of your choice.
- If you do not like curry, I would use turmeric instead for the yellow colour.
- You can use any stock here, whether it is beef or vegetable stock. If you don’t have any of these, use water that has been seasoned with salt.
