## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cups white vinegar
- [ ] 1 cup diced green bell pepper (with seeds)
- [ ] 1 cup diced red bell pepper (no seeds)
- [ ] 1/3 cup diced chilli manzano (or any hot/sweet pepper)
- [ ] 1 serrano pepper diced (with seeds)
- [ ] 1/4 cup finely minced onion
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground allspice
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cumin (you may wish to add another 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- [ ] 1 teaspoon white sugar
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Add the above ingredients to a medium-sized pot and bring to boil.
2. Once it is boiling, reduce to a high simmer.
3. Stir in 1/8 teaspoon of xanthan gum and continue to simmer.
4. Add 1 cup of water as the liquid reduces and continue doing this for about 4 hours.
5. Then, allow the relish to reduce to the desired thickness.
