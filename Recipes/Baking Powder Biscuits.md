## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cups all-purpose flour
- [ ] 4 teaspoons baking powder
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- [ ] 3/4 cup milk

## Instructions

1. Add liquids to dry ingredients.
2. mix quickly to form a soft dough.
3. Turn out and knead gently until smooth (10-12 times).
4. Roll to 1/2 or 3/4 inch thickness.
5. Cut with a cutter.
6. Bake 12–20 minutes at 425° F.
