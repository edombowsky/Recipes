## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons paprika
- [ ] 2 tablespoons garlic powder
- [ ] 2 tablespoons dried oregano

- [ ] 1 tablespoon onion powder

## Instructions

In a medium bowl, mix together all ingredients. Use as desired.
