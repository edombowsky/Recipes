## Ingredients

- [ ] ¼ cup olive oil
- [ ] ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- [ ] 2–3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- [ ] 1 clove minced garlic
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

In a small bowl or jar with a lid, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, kosher salt, and black pepper. Whisk or shake until all the ingredients are well incorporated.
