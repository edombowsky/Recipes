## Ingredients

- [ ] ½ cup mayonnaise
- [ ] ½ cup sour cream
- [ ] ½ cup buttermilk
- [ ] 3 tablespoons minced parsley
- [ ] 2 tablespoons minced dill
- [ ] 1 garlic clove grated or minced
- [ ] 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] Kosher salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and whisk together. Taste and adjust seasonings accordingly. Transfer to a resealable container and refrigerate until ready to use.
