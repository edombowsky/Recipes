## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup warm water (200 ml)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon yeast (10 grams)
- [ ] 4 teaspoons sugar
- [ ] 1/3 cup vegetable oil (60 ml)
- [ ] 1 cup warm milk (200 ml)
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 5 cups of flour (600 gr) + ~30-50 gr to work with dough
- [ ] 100 gr butter

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the first 4 ingredients. Cover with plastic wrap for 10 mins.
2. Dissolve salt in warm milk.
3. Add milk to yeast mixture and stir.
4. Sift 5 cups of flour into the mixture.
5. Fold flour into liquid yeast mixture.
6. Form dough into a ball.
7. Let stand for 45 mins for the dough to rise.
8. Punch it down.
9. Spread flour on counter top.
10. Transfer dough to counter and split into 12 pieces.
11. Add a bit more flour if dough becomes sticky.
12. Make balls of the 12 pieces.
13. Divide 100 grams of butter into 8 parts. The butter should be soft and at room temperature.
14. Place 8 pieces of butter on top of 8 of the dough balls.
15. Work balls into disks with your hands, stacking two of the buttered ones on top of each other, end with one without butter.
16. Form these into balls then flatten out into disks.
17. Place into a hot cast iron frying pan.
18. Cook each on each side for about 3 minutes.
19. Use a toothpick to release air from the bread as it cooks.
20. Bread is ready when both sides are a nice golden brown.
21. Place on a rack to cool.
