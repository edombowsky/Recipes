## Ingredients

### Sauce

- [ ] 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
- [ ] 1/4 cup peanut butter
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- [ ] 1/4 cup water (*approximate*)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chilli crunch
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dark soy
- [ ] Juice of one lime

### Main Dish

- [ ] Oil
- [ ] 1 pound of your favourite steak, sliced into thin bites
- [ ] 1/2 large yellow onions
- [ ] 1/2 large red pepper
- [ ] Kosher salt
- [ ] 2 packs instant ramen noodles
- [ ] Diced green onion & sesame seeds for garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine sauce ingredients in a small bow, mix well and set aside
2. Heat about a tablespoon of oil in a large pan and when just starting to smoke, add the beef
3. Cook, stirring constantly until just done, probably no more than a minute – remove and set aside
4. Return pan to the heat, add a bit more oil and then the onion and pepper – cook until just softened, 3–4 minutes
5. While the vegetables are cooking, add the noodles to a pot of boiling water
6. Add the beef and any juices to the veggies, give a good stir, then add the drained noodles
7. Mix well, add the sauce and continue stirring to combine, season to taste with salt and after about a minute when the sauce is thickened and everything is coated…we can eat. I mean you can eat
