## Ingredients

### Ingredients for Spice Mixture

- [ ] 1 teaspoon paprika
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dried basil
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dried sage
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dried coriander
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon oregano
- [ ] 1 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sea salt

### Ingredients for the Gravy

- [ ] 2 tablespoons or 28g butter
- [ ] 2 tablespoons or 16g flour
- [ ] 237 ml or 1 cup chicken stock
- [ ] 355 ml or 1.5 cups brown veal or beef stock
- [ ] 1 tablespoon of the spice mixture
- [ ] 1 tablespoon of the secret ingredient shown in the video (white wine vinegar)

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a frying pan
2. Add the flour and cook for about 1 minute to make a roux and remove the flour taste.
3. Stir in the chicken stock and brown veal stock and fully combine the roux.
4. Add in 1 tablespoon of the 11 herbs and spice mixture. The secret ingredient (white wine vinegar) is added now.
5. Reduce the gravy by about half over medium heat for about 20 minutes.
6. Once gravy has reduced to the desired amount and has thickened up it is ready to be served.
