Easy homemade Chicken Lettuce Wraps. These are low in carbs but loaded with protein so it'll keep you full for hours without all the guilt! Great for a light lunch or dinner.

## Ingredients

### Chicken Filling

- [ ] 2 tablespoons oil
- [ ] 8 baby [bella mushrooms](https://www.shroomer.com/baby-bella-mushrooms/), finely chopped (see notes)
- [ ] 1 pound (ca. 454 g) ground chicken (turkey or beef)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- [ ] 1 small chopped shallot (or 1/2 red onion)

### Sauce

- [ ] 1 tablespoon EACH: toasted sesame oil, hoisin sauce, sriracha sauce, AND rice wine vinegar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons EACH: low sodium soy sauce AND oyster sauce
- [ ] 1 1/2 tablespoon grated ginger
- [ ] 1 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar

## Instructions

1. **CHICKEN FILLING:** Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a medium frying-pan over medium high heat. Add the mushrooms and sauté them for 3–4 minutes or until the mushrooms reduce to half their size. Remove to a bowl.
2. **SAUCE:** Combine the ingredients for the sauce in a mason jar. Screw on the lid and give it a good shake until all the ingredients combine, set aside for later.
3. **CHICKEN FILLING CONTINUED:** Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil to the frying-pan and cook the shallots over medium high heat for 1 minute before adding in the garlic. Let the garlic cook for 30 seconds before adding in the ground chicken. Break the lumps in the ground chicken using a wooden spoon and let the chicken cook for 5–7 minutes or until it cooks all the way through. Add the mushrooms back into the frying-pan and drizzle with the prepared sauce. Let cook for 1–2 minutes. Allow the filling to cool for a few minutes before filling in butter lettuce leaves. Top with sesame seeds and crushed peanuts.

## Notes

- Leftover filling can be kept in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
- You can add in 1 (4-6 ounce) can of chopped water chestnuts when you add the sauce to the chicken if you want them to taste more like P.F. Changs lettuce wraps.
