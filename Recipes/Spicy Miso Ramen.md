## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons oil, divided
- [ ] 1/2 lb ground chicken or tofu
- [ ] 8 oz shiitake mushrooms
- [ ] 1 shallot, minced
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1-inch ginger, grated
- [ ] 7 cups broth (chicken/vegetable)
- [ ] 4 tablespoons white miso paste
- [ ] 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon chilli oil
- [ ] 3/4 lb dry ramen noodles

## Instructions

1. Cook chicken, set aside.
2. Sauté mushrooms, shallots, garlic, and ginger.
3. Add broth and simmer 10 mins.
4. Mix miso paste, add to broth.
5. Cook noodles separately and combine.
