## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup sour cream
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried chopped onion
- [ ] 1 teaspoon onion powder
- [ ] 1 pinch garlic powder
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] 1 Tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley, or 1 teaspoon dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Place sour cream in a small bowl. Add dried onion, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and parsley. Mix together very well until completely uniform. Taste with a chip and adjust seasoning if necessary.
2. Refrigerate for 1 hour to allow the flavours to meld and the dried onion to soften. Store in the refrigerator for 5 to 7 days.
