## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 1/2 pounds ground round
- [ ] 1 cup diced onions
- [ ] 1 cup diced zucchini
- [ ] 1/2 cup diced okra
- [ ] 1 cup cubed potatoes
- [ ] 1 cup sliced carrots
- [ ] 1/2 cup diced celery
- [ ] 1 cup shredded cabbage
- [ ] 1-15 ounce tin tomatoes
- [ ] 1/4 cup rice or 1/2 cup macaroni elbow noodles
- [ ] 1 1/2 quarts (6 cups) water
- [ ] 1 bay leaf
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon thyme
- [ ] 3 teaspoons salt (more or less to taste)
- [ ] ground black pepper to taste
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 1/c cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Brown ground round in a large kettle.
2. Add vegetables, water, and spices and bring to a boil.
3. Sprinkle rice (or noodles) into the mixture. Cover and simmer for at least one hour.
4. Sprinkle with grated cheese to serve.
