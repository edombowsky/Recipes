## Ingredients

- [ ] 30 mL (2 tablespoons) olive oil
- [ ] 3 onions, sliced thinly
- [ ] 5 mL (1 teaspoon) ground cumin
- [ ] 560 mL (2 ¼ cups) brown or wild rice, cooked
- [ ] 1 can (540 mL/19 oz) brown or green lentils, drained and rinsed

## Instructions

1. In a pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add onions and cook until they turn dark caramel brown, stirring often. This will take about 15 minutes. Remove half of the onions to a bowl (these are for garnish later). Sprinkle in ground cumin and cook about 1 minute more.
2. Add cooked rice to onions and often stir for 3 minutes. Stir in lentils and cook for another 2 minutes.
3. Serve rice and lentils with reserved caramelized onions on top.

## Notes

- You will know onions are caramelized when they have a deep chestnut colour and are slightly crispy. Add a little water to onions if they are sticking to the bottom of the pan.
- Using dry rice and lentils? Use 190 mL (¾ cup) dry rice and 250 mL (1 cup) dry lentils, and cook according to package directions before adding at step.
