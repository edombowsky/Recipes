## Ingredients

- [ ] 3/4 cup butter
- [ ] 2 1/2 cups chopped onions
- [ ] 2 cups chopped celery (add red pepper)
- [ ] 4 teaspoons crushed dried sage
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1 1/4 teaspoons crushed dried marjoram
- [ ] 1 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried thyme
- [ ] 14 cups homemade-style bread, in 1/2 inch (1 cm) cubes
- [ ] 1 cup finely chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. In a large frying-pan, melt butter. Cook onions, celery, sage, salt, savoury, marjoram, pepper, and thyme over low heat, stirring frequently, until vegetables are translucent, 10 to 15 minutes
2. Place bread in a large bowl. Add onion mixture along with parsley. Toss well. Taste and adjust seasoning
3. Makes enough to stuffing for one 16 to 18 pound (7.3 to 8.2 kg) bird.
