## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/4 cup lemon juice
- [ ] 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
- [ ] 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- [ ] 1/2 cup olive oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 teaspoons chives, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in a medium jar with a tight-fitting lid, and shake to combine.
2. Can keep in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Shake well before serving.
