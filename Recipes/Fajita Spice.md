## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 teaspoon cumin
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon chilli powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 teaspoon oregano
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon sugar`

## Instructions

1. Mix together and store in an airtight jar.
2. When cooking your fajitas, add 1/2 teaspoon of homemade fajita seasoning per person towards the end of cooking.
