What makes this fresh green bean salad so good is that the beans soak up the vinaigrette while they are still warm for great flavour.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- [ ] ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- [ ] ⅙ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] ½ small sweet onion, thinly sliced
- [ ] ½ clove garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- [ ] ½ teaspoon white sugar
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
- [ ] freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil over high heat. Add green beans and cook, uncovered, until soft but still firm to the bite, 7 to 10 minutes. Often Check so beans don't overcook.
2. Meanwhile, whisk vinegar, olive oil, onion, garlic, parsley, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
3. Drain green beans and mix with dressing while still warm. Allow to marinate at room temperature for at least 1 hour. Serve at room temperature or chilled.
