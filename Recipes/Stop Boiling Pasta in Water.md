## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 lb (ca. 454 g) white button cap mushrooms
- [ ] 1 container oyster and portobello mushrooms
- [ ] pinch salt
- [ ] pinch pepper
- [ ] 1 cup vegetable broth
- [ ] 1 cup water
- [ ] 3 small shallots
- [ ] 3 or 4 cloves of garlic
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] 1 1/2 cup pasta
- [ ] 1/2 cup Pinot Grigio
- [ ] 2 cups mushroom broth
- [ ] 2 tablespoons vegan butter
- [ ] rosemary
- [ ] chives

## Instructions

1. Wash mushrooms in cold water, then roughly chop them.
2. In large sauté pan over medium heat add mushrooms, salt, vegetable oil and water. Bring to simmer and allow them to cook for 15 minutes.
3. Slice up shallots, then smash and mince garlic.
4. Strain mushrooms, save the broth.
5. In the same hot pan add olive oil then the shallots. Turn the heat down to a medium/low and cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add in garlic and toss everything around. Add in pasta, then the mushrooms and liquids. Pinot Grigio (let cook alcohol away for a moment), then the mushroom broth.
7. Once pasta is cooked through, add salt, pepper, vegan butter, chives, and rosemary. Stir everything up, then plate! Enjoy!
