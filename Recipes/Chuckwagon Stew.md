## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped celery
- [ ] 1/4 cup chopped onion
- [ ] 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
- [ ] 1 pound (ca. 454 g) lean minced beef
- [ ] 1 16 oz can baked beans
- [ ] 1/4 cup ketchup
- [ ] 1/4 cup water
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
2. Toss celery, onion, and green pepper into the bottom of the baking dish. Crumble minced beef on top and bake uncovered for 20 mins.
3. Remove from oven, stir in remaining ingredients, return to oven until heated through.

### Note

1. It may be topped with biscuits made according to Bisquick, etc and baked for another 15 mins.
2. **Taken from:** SOMETHING SPECIAL Cook book For College Kids (No Prerequisites!) Paperback – January 1, 1981, by Sheila McDougall (Author) ISBN: OCLC:426228240
